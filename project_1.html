More about me


My name is Carl Strümpfer. My surname is German, although I am not, and it translates to ‘socks’, which
I coincidentally enjoy wearing on cold days. I live on the very bottom of the African continent, in
South Africa.

I am also currently a Multimedia Design student at the Stellenbosch Academy of Design and Photography,
which to conserve oxygen, we all simply call the Academy. What you see here is some of the cool stuff
that I’ve made there.


I find myself intrigued by the concept of light, and how it dances around objects and plays with the world
around us. I make it my mission in life to capture the game between light and darkness, by any means
possible, be it photo, video or film.

I can keep myself busy with my myriad of interests, that range from photography to restoring old
technological relics, like keyboards and reel-to-reel tape decks. I also suffer from a severe case of
retro 80’s nostalgia, which influences much of what I own and listen to.

Lumen

Outline

A short film is a perfect example of how artistry can be condensed into an easily consumable format , while still retaining the depth of a longer piece. Several core techniques and dynamics of animation were fused into creating an animated short film. A character was created and developed with all the richness and personality that it could muster.

The character was then woven into a story, itself crafted using a variety of story-writing skills and rules. In order to then fully realise the story, a hand-crafted environment was created in order to set the stage upon which it can run its course.

Execution

The storyline relied heavily on the concept of light and how it interacts with surroundings. Conventional 2D animation was not able to deliver the required lighting, and so 3D environments were crafted for this purpose. The 3D approach also allowed for greater control over shots and angles. The final execution is a hybrid of 2D character animation matted against a 3D background.

Plot

The film tells the story of a lighthouse keeper and his friend, a small puff of light. On one particular day, the lighthouse’s light mysteriously dies, and they are tasked with fixing it as soon as possible, but not without personal sacrifice.
